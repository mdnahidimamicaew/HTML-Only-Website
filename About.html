Brief Story

In 2016, three fervent technopreneurs, Md Nahid Imam, Faeez M Noor, and Jeff McDonald, united to use blockchain technologies in order to change the world for the best. They quickly realized that the luxury consumer goods sector seemed just the right fit: counterfeit was spreading, and brand trust was dropping. In 2017, Bloain was born. Since then, LuxTag has had incredible growth in the last years, thanks to the commitment of a diverse and passionate team, to a community that supports the project, and thanks to those that approach innovation and technology with an open mind.



Our Visions

1.1 An honest world built upon innovation, integrity and cooperation. 1.2 LuxTag enables assurances to supersede assumptions.



Our Mission

We redefine the concept of authenticity, ownership and data governance in the digital age, using high-end technologies such as DLT/Blockchain and IOT.
